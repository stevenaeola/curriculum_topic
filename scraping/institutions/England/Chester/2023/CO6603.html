  • Tutorials.

  • Online learning as directed.
  • Demonstrations and practical exercises.
  • Formative feedback will be given during contact time, by email and via discussion boards where appropriate.

By the end of the module students will be able to:

  1. Critically demonstrate the application of covert and discrete command and control structures.
  2. Formulate technical proposals for incident response based on a given scenario.
  3. Explore operational techniques to provide attribution details on a cyber attack.
  4. Formulate a strategic plan to empower an entity to deal with emerging threats.
  5. Appraise key tactical indicators from incident reports and make adjustments accordingly.
